About US

Fresh fruits is a fresh initiative in the area of Fresh Fruits,vegetables and Dry Fruits sales Founded in 2018, idea behind this portal is to deliver the best available products at best possible rates to our customers. We are going to be only tech-driven fresh produce company offering linkages to wide array of services related to sector – right from farm to kitchen in fruit and vegetable sector. The Dashboard provides support to fresh fruit’ (starts with fresh apples) sellers and buyers to connect and match their supplies and demand, and drive the process of negotiation, deal settlement. Both Buyer and Seller can use the search engine to find out about the offer/needs of the other side. Buyers can search for multiple fruits/variaties and use the search results to reach out to the Buyers and initiate the interaction.
